Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which coresponds to the day in the week they were born. These 'day names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their bith order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
